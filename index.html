Three summers of research.

Through MSU's Summer Research Opportunities Program.

Approximate Symmetry via the Euler Characteristic Transform

May–Jul 2025 · MSU CMSE

Built a topology-flavored framework for detecting bilateral and rotational symmetry in 2D binary shapes. ECTs, L1 distance curves, Fourier spectral analysis, polar plots and heatmaps — Python pipelines end-to-end. First place at the AGEP Student Success Conference.

Impact of Opportunity Zones in Michigan

May–Jul 2022 + 2024 · MSU Economics

Tract-level longitudinal dataset of Michigan communities (ACS '12–'22). Independently implemented DiD and fixed-effects in R and Stata to estimate effects of the 2017 TCJA Opportunity Zone designation. Returned in '24 with updated data, demographic controls, and lagged-effect tests. Second place AGEP poster + MEA Conference oral.

Automated Retinal Vessel Segmentation (SWAN)

May 2023–Feb 2024 · OSU Graduate School

Built a U-Net pipeline (Python + MATLAB) with custom data augmentation for an NIH-funded ophthalmic diagnostics study. Hand-annotated training masks for an unlabeled dataset; drafted a first-author manuscript pending revision.
